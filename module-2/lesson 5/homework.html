Learn the Box Model

Margin is the space outside the border.
It creates space between boxes.
Border is the line around the box.
It wraps the padding and content.
Padding is the space inside the border.
It pushes the content inside.
Content is the stuff inside the box.
Like text or images.
The box model is how websites control spacing and layout. Every box has margin, border, padding, and content. Learning this helps you build better webpages!
